Classic 3D room

"A classic 3D room modeled in Blender, combining vintage furniture with retro gaming elements. This project reflects my passion for timeless interior design and nostalgic gaming aesthetics, blending both into a cozy and immersive scene. The carefully chosen furniture, decor, and color palette create an authentic classic atmosphere while incorporating subtle gaming touches for a unique contrast."
